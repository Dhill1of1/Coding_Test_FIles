Menu

Double Double

A double double is a burger with two patties, two slices of cheese, and two slices of bacon.

Cheeseburger

A cheeseburger is a burger with a slice of cheese on top of the burger.

Hamburger

A hamburger is a burger with a single patty and no cheese.

French Fries

French fries are potatoes cut and fried in oil.

Beverages

Beverages are drinks you can get from the drink machine.

Shakes

Shakes are a type of drink that is made with ice cream and milk. We have vanilla, chocolate, and strawberry.
